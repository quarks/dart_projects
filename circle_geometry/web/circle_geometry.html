Circle geometry

Circle - line intersection

Press and drag the mouse to define the line. Clicking the mouse will create a random line.

If both ends of the line is within the circle there is no intersection. If one end of the line is inside and the other outside the circle then there is a single intersection point.

If both ends of the line are outside the circle then there meybe two intersection points, a single intersection point (line is a tangent) or no intersection points.

Intersection points are show as red dots.

Tangents to a circle from a point

Tangents between circles

The green circle will be centered about the mouse as it moves.

The lines between the circles are tangents to both circles. If the circles do not intersect then there are 4 tangents, if they intersect then there are just 2, the outer tangents. If one circle completely covers the other circle then there are no tangents.

When two circles intersect then the intersecion points are shown as red cicles. Normally there will be two intersection points but if the circles are just touching then there will be just one.
